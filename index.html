About Team Vimalakar Foundation

Team Vimalakar Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to providing free healthcare, education, and empowerment programs to underprivileged communities across India. Founded and led by Dr. Vimalakar Reddy Eppa, HOD & Sr. Consultant in GI Surgery at KIMS-SUNSHINE Hospitals, the foundation is backed by a team of expert gastroenterologists, surgeons, and healthcare professionals who strive to make a lasting impact on the lives of those who lack access to essential medical services.
