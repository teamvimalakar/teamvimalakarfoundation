Kunjara Yatra

Kunjara Yatra is our flagship healthcare initiative focused on GI Cancer Screening, raising awareness about diet, lifestyle modifications, and preventive healthcare. Through these camps, we aim to serve underprivileged communities with essential medical care and health education.

Services Provided

GI Cancer Screening

Early detection and prevention of gastrointestinal cancers through comprehensive screening programs.

Dietary Counseling

Personalized nutritional guidance to promote digestive health and prevent GI disorders.

Lifestyle Modification

Guidance on lifestyle changes to reduce risk factors associated with GI cancers and improve overall health.

Health Education

Community awareness programs on preventive healthcare and early symptom recognition.

Medical Consultations

One-on-one consultations with healthcare professionals for personalized care and advice.

Follow-up Care

Ongoing support and referrals for patients requiring additional medical attention.

Our Kunjara Sainyam

Dr. Rahul Sharma

Medical Director

Led the founding of Kunjara Yatra initiative with expertise in gastroenterology. Has personally overseen all 13 medical camps and performed over 500 screenings.

Dr. Priya Patel

Nutrition Specialist

Developed comprehensive dietary guidelines for GI health. Has conducted 25+ workshops on preventive nutrition and led dietary counseling for over 1,000 participants.

Dr. Vijay Kumar

Screening Coordinator

Specialized in early detection protocols. Has trained 45 healthcare workers and established standardized screening procedures used across all camp locations.

Anjali Reddy

Community Outreach Manager

Coordinated with local communities to ensure maximum participation. Has established partnerships with 30+ village councils and mobilized over 2,000 participants.

Dr. Suresh Menon

Medical Research Lead

Analyzes health data collected from camps to improve future interventions. Has published 3 research papers on rural GI health based on Kunjara Yatra findings.

Lakshmi Devi

Patient Support Coordinator

Manages follow-up care and referrals for patients requiring additional treatment. Has coordinated care for 350+ patients identified with significant health concerns.
